About Me

about me

Jibin started his career with INK talks (An Indian version of Ted Talks) where he was an intern and coordinator for Teenovators Project and was handling their day to day operations. He is of a multi-disciplinary person and realistic in approach. He has varied interests in Science, engineering in the fields like electronics, computer science, robotics, programming etc. He is a volunteer with IEEE (World’s largest technical organization) and has made lot of value connections. His works with IEEE, opened up his dreams to do things that matters in the most unique way possible. He has experience in organising over 150 events including global competitions, conferences, and student congresses.

Jibin Sabu completed his B.tech from FISAT (Mahatma Gandhi University affiliated). During his UG programme, he worked with AIESEC – (Association internationale des étudiants en sciences économiques et commerciales) an International Association of students in Economic and commercial science, MAD (Make A Difference) etc.

In his free time he likes to mingle a lot of with people, he is energetic about roller skating, table tennis, travel, meeting people and also if time permits he jumps himself into playing basketball. He worked with Profoundis Labs which got acquired by Fullcontact in August 2016 and have been part of Sales team till June 2017.

He is currently working with Agrima Infotech (Recipebook.io) in Marketing and Partner Acquisition.
